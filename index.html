Ugali with Mayai on the side

Ugali is a staple food in many African countries, made from maize flour and water. It is often served with various side dishes.
Mayai, or fried eggs, can be a delicious accompaniment.

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Gradually add maize flour while stirring to avoid lumps.
  3. Cook until the mixture thickens and pulls away from the sides of the pot.
  4. In a separate pan, heat oil and fry the eggs until cooked to your liking.
  5. Serve ugali with fried eggs on the side.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • Vegetables (optional)

Tips

  • For a richer flavor, add spices or vegetables to the ugali.
  • Serve with a side of vegetables or meat for a complete meal.

Nutrition Information

Serving Size:1 plate

Calories:400

Protein:20g

Carbohydrates:60g

Fat:10g

Fiber:5g

Sugar:2g

Sodium:200mg

Serving Suggestions

Ugali can be served with a variety of sides such as vegetables, meat stews, or sauces. It pairs well with sukuma wiki (collard greens) or any other vegetable dish.

Enjoy your meal!
